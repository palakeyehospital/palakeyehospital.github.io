Welcome to Palak Eye Clinic!

Akshar Eye Clinic's relationship with success began in the year 1990 when Akshar eye clinic was born by the untiring efforts of Dr. Nitin Malkan along with Mrs. Geeta Malkan as a small two-bedded hospital and a day care centre for ophthalmic practice.

Our centre comprises of comfortable and homely recovery rooms, state of the art laboratory and a separate operation theatre complex with air locked lobbies. All the surgeries are assisted by qualified and experienced ophthalmic anaesthetists. Each subspecialty is attended by the respective qualified ophthalmic surgeon. Three anterior segment surgeons available round the clock for providing total eye care throughout the day.

With Regards,
Dr. Nitin Shah
